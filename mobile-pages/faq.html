Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

FAQ

Q: What is this application about?

A: This application is designed to facilitate communication and collaboration between users.

Q: Is the application free to use?

A: Yes, the basic features are free. However, premium features may require a subscription.

Q: How do I create an account?

A: Click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill in your details.

Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?

A: Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

Q: How do I send a message?

A: Open a chat room, type your message in the input field, and press Enter.

Q: Can I delete a message?

A: Yes, click on the message and select "Delete" from the options.

Q: How do I create a new room?

A: Click on the "Create Room" button and follow the instructions.

Q: Can I invite users to a room?

A: Yes, use the "Invite" option in the room settings to send invitations.

Q: Can I create multiple rooms?

A: Yes, you can create as many rooms as you want. Each room can have a unique name and purpose to suit your needs.

Q: How do I manage my rooms?

A: You can manage your rooms by accessing the "My Rooms" section in the app. There, you can edit room names, descriptions, and privacy settings.

Q: Can I make a room private?

A: Yes, you can set a room to private in the settings. Private rooms require an invitation or approval for others to join.

Q: Is there a limit to the number of users in a room?

A: No, there is no fixed limit on the number of users in a room. However, we recommend keeping the number manageable for better interactions.

Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes, we use end-to-end encryption to ensure your data is protected.

Q: Can I control who sees my profile?

A: Yes, you can adjust your privacy settings in the account settings section.

Q: Can I use the app on multiple devices?

A: Yes, our app is compatible with mobile phones and tablets, and we are actively working on a desktop version to enhance your experience across all devices.

Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes, we use end-to-end encryption to ensure your data is protected. Additionally, our app complies with the latest data protection regulations to keep your personal information safe.

Q: Can I control who sees my profile?

A: Yes, you can adjust your privacy settings in the account settings section. You can decide who can view your profile, send you friend requests, and join your chat rooms.

Q: How can I provide feedback about the app?

A: You can use the "Feedback" section in the app settings to share your thoughts or suggestions. Your input helps us improve the app.

Q: How can I contact support?

A: You can reach our support team via the "Support" section in the app or by emailing us at support@ourapp.com.
